Refund Policy for Site Craft Hub

At Site Craft Hub, we strive to provide high-quality services to our clients. However, we understand that there may be instances where you are not fully satisfied with the services you receive. This Refund Policy outlines the terms under which we process refunds and exchanges. By purchasing our services, you agree to the following terms.

1. Refund Eligibility

Refunds are considered under the following circumstances:

  • Non-delivery of Service: If we fail to deliver the services you purchased within the agreed-upon timeframe, you may be eligible for a full refund.
  • Service Defects: If the service provided does not meet the standards agreed upon or does not function as expected (for example, if the website developed by us contains major errors that cannot be fixed within a reasonable period), you may request a refund.
  • Account Termination by Site Craft Hub: If we terminate your account or services due to a violation of our Terms and Conditions, you will not be eligible for a refund.

2. Non-Refundable Services

The following services are generally non-refundable:

  • Hosting Services: As we are not a direct hosting provider and purchase hosting services on behalf of our clients from third-party providers, refunds for hosting-related charges are subject to the policies of the hosting providers we use.
  • Domain Name Registration: Once a domain name is registered, it cannot be refunded.
  • Completed Services: If a service (such as web development or design) has been completed to your specifications or a final version has been delivered, no refunds will be issued.
  • Customization and Development Work: Any work done in customization, development, or design that has been started or completed cannot be refunded once initiated.

3. How to Request a Refund

If you believe you are eligible for a refund, please contact us at sitecrafthub@gmail.com. Please include the following details in your refund request:

  • Your name and contact information
  • A description of the service you purchased
  • The reason for the refund request
  • Any supporting documentation, such as screenshots or correspondence, that demonstrates your eligibility for a refund

We aim to process refund requests within 7 business days of receipt. If your request is approved, we will initiate the refund through the original payment method.

4. Partial Refunds

In some cases, a partial refund may be granted. This can happen if part of the service has been delivered or partially completed, and a full refund is not feasible.

5. Refund Processing Time

If your refund request is approved, please note that the processing time will depend on the payment method used:

  • Credit/Debit Card Payments: Refunds typically take 5-10 business days to process.
  • PayPal Payments: Refunds can be processed within 2-5 business days.
  • Bank Transfers: Refunds via bank transfer may take up to 10 business days.

6. Exceptions to the Refund Policy

In certain circumstances, Site Craft Hub reserves the right to make exceptions to this Refund Policy. If you feel that your case warrants an exception, please reach out to us, and we will review your request on a case-by-case basis.

7. Changes to the Refund Policy

Site Craft Hub reserves the right to amend or modify this Refund Policy at any time. Any changes will be reflected on this page, and the updated date will be posted at the top. Please review this page periodically to stay informed about our refund process.
